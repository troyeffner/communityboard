## Story

Community Board is 100% transparent and 100% live.

When users subscribe, they see everything: live income, where it goes (servers, vendors,
operations — itemized), what's left in the community pool, and where their specific
contribution is flowing. Then they direct it — toward the product area, job to be done,
or outcome they want prioritized.

This is not a feature. It's the product soul. The community board is a project
subscribers co-fund, in public, in real time. Radical transparency is the differentiator.
