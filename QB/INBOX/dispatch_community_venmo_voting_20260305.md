## Story

Subscribers vote by sending money to fractal nodes.

Not a slider. Not abstract allocation. A dollar sent to a specific target:
- User Jobs — work that affects their direct experience
- Business Tree Jobs — branch-level product work
- Business Trunk Jobs — core platform (NDD, FWM dispatch, routing)
- Business Roots Jobs — foundation/infrastructure work (invisible but essential)

"As long as you are working" — subscribers can fund roots they'll never see, trusting the method is running. That's a patron relationship, not a SaaS subscription.

---

## Fractal Targets

Each target = a node in the FWM tree. Funding flows down from where it lands to the Contractor executing that node.

```
Subscriber sends $5 to "Business Trunk Jobs"
  → NDD/FWM platform work gets funded signal
  → Coach routes to highest-IR trunk node in progress
  → Contractor executing that node gets attributed funding
  → Subscriber sees: "Your $5 is funding [name] — working on [job]."
```
