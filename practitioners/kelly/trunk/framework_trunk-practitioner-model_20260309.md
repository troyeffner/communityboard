## What Was Established

**The dual-edge structure (wow_98)**

Each practitioner owns a personal trunk workspace with two directories:

- `soil/` — creator edge. What this practitioner has built, decided, and proven. Growth rings. Trunk contributions in raw form.
- `ether/` (alias: `external/`) — consumer edge. What this practitioner picks up from the world in their specific discipline. Sorted by field and need.

The mesh between any two practitioners' fractals — when both are populated — is where new seeds are generated inside the trunk. This is not metaphor. It is the actual mechanism.

**The loop closes and creates new soil (wow_99)**

Practitioners are ephemeral context-bound units. The cycle is:
1. Pick up resources from the world
2. Carry them into the trunk
3. Expressions fall outward from the trunk
4. Expressions complete and return — visible in space
5. The act of closing the loop generates new soil

Soil is not the beginning of the lifecycle. It is the product of completed cycles.

**Compression follows rings, not direct pressure (wow_81, wow_84)**

The trunk does not compress soil directly. Soil becomes roots through growth ring cycles — when enough raw material accumulates and a ring-building pass is run, the soil compresses upward. Higher stages (Trunk, Branches, Tips) look down and create new visibility into the material below. Attempting to compress prematurely produces brittle structure.
