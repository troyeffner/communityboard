## The Decoder (9 Structural Tags)

These tags compiled upward from the prior instantiation. They survived because they recurred, held under pressure, and proved structural across contexts:

### 1. Community Convergence Signal
Money as voting signal. Dollars flow fractally to any node (job, narrative, contractor) where they land. No separation between funding and signal. Internal blocker votes + external community votes converge on the same node.

### 2. Intelligence Engine
Practice-derived evidence becomes accumulated intelligence through continuous hypothesis-testing-and-reconciliation cycles across distributed projects. Schema-agnostic. Digests workflow experiments, templates, heuristics from real execution. Surfaces patterns.

### 3. Home Ground / Common Ground
Two co-equal structural layers. Home Ground: private, personal practice. Common Ground: public, community-collective. Optional friction-gated crossings between them. Individual agency and collective contribution co-exist without forcing either direction.

### 4. Cross-Layer Completion
The community becomes the execution engine. A recipient builds two layers of a fractal. The community QB executes the next layer and returns the work within the recipient's existing context. Structural continuity, not random surprise.

### 5. Playing in Practice
Learning embeds in mechanics through play, not front-loaded instruction. Products reveal complexity progressively. Users discover rules through interaction. Applies to onboarding, scaffolding, AI integration (scaffold not crutch), research judgment building.

### 6. Distributed Experiment Orchestration
One hypothesis, multiple exercise deployments across multiple projects. Community members complete hypothesis-tied exercises. Responses feed shared schema. System reconciles across distributed sources simultaneously. Insights loop back.

### 7. Community Governance Loop
Subscribers direct real dollars (not abstract votes) to specific JTBD categories. Allocation is live, transparent. Aggregate voting surfaces what community empirically wants built. Subscribers become micro-QBs for product direction.

### 8. Self-Governing Wage Layer
Contractors collectively govern wage standards. Union membership, not individual negotiation. Structural ownership of the fraction they create. Union standing becomes public trust signal and internal routing priority.

### 9. Visibility Bridge
The full loop is visible: Coach to QB to Contractor to Subscriber and back. Subscriber patronage traces to named Contractor, to their impact trail, to their union standing. Transforms subscriber from abstract funder to patron. Contractor from anonymous labor to community figure.
