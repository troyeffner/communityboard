# Leaves

This folder is the external surface of communityboard-trunk. Every file here represents something this tree has grown to completion and shipped beyond the forest: live community features, shipped interfaces, public-facing product content.

Leaves only face outward. Internal deliverables between practitioners and trees do not pass through leaves. Those route through TRUNK and land directly as soil in the receiving tree, with a lightweight trace fiber logged in the sending tree's soil.

## Growth Model

Leaves are not authored. They are grown.

A leaf begins as a bud at the lowest layer of the tree and pushes upward through every layer. As the bud passes through each layer, every checkpoint at that layer fires: content standards, governance rules, project constraints, practitioner discipline. By the time the bud emerges here as a visible leaf, it has been evaluated by the full fractal pipeline.

A leaf carries traceable lineage back to its root. If you cannot trace it, it was not grown properly and does not belong here. A leaf reflects real-time internal accuracy. If the structure beneath it changes, the leaf either updates or falls. A leaf that looks wrong externally is a diagnostic signal: trace back down through the layers to find where growth broke. No separate QA step exists at the leaf level because the growth process is the quality process.

## Lifecycle

1. Content grows through the tree from soil upward, passing through every checkpoint at every layer.
2. The bud emerges in leaves/ as a file representing what is live externally.
3. The leaf stays here as long as the content is active in the world.
4. When the content is retired, replaced, or pulled back, the leaf falls to soil/ as a decomposition fiber carrying context about why it fell.

## Relationship to Other Surfaces

**Soil** feeds growth. Fallen leaves return here. Cross-tree internal deliverables also land directly in soil (they never become leaves).

**TRUNK** is the transport layer for internal routing between practitioners and trees. TRUNK moves material. Leaves ship material to the world. These are separate paths.

**Ether** holds knowledge that left active circulation but was never shipped externally.

**Leaves** is the only surface that represents what this tree has live outside the forest.
